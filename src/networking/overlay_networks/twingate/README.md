## Getting started

### Registration
Twingate is limited but free for personal use.

1. Navigatge to [Twingate's pricing page](https://www.twingate.com/pricing)
2. Flip the toggle to the `Personal` mode
3. Click the `Get Started` button under the `Free` plan
4. Enter the email address your going to use to register and click `Get Started`
