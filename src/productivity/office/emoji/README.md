💥 The "Splat" (Collision)
 * Collision (💥) — The most popular choice for a "splat" or impact. It has a jagged, splash-like shape and is usually yellow or orange.


🎨 Colors & Splashes
 * Artist Palette (🎨) — Often used in combination with other symbols to imply paint.
 * Sweat Droplets (💦) — The best representation of a blue splash or "splatter."
 * Drop of Blood (🩸) — Frequently used for a red paint drop or splat.
 * Droplet (💧) — A single, clean blue drop.
 * Soap (🧼) — In some emoji sets, this has a "sudsy" or splashy look.


🌑 The "Ink Blot"
 * New Moon (🌑) — Often used as a black "ink blot" or a large, dark splat.
 * Nazar Amulet (🧿) — Sometimes used for a blue-centered splash or "eye" of paint.

🟢 The "Color Drops"
Many developers use the solid colored circles to represent specific paint splats or color swatches:

* Sparkles (✨) — The universal symbol for AI and "magic" code generation.
* Magic Wand (🪄) — Often used to represent the "one-shot" generation power of Claude.
* Glowing Star (🌟) — A clean, bright symbol of intelligence.
